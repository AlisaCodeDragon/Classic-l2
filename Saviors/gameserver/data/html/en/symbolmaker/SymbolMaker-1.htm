All symbols improve one characteristic and worsen another. This achieves a kind of harmony.
The character has three physical characteristics - Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, and three mental - Intelligence, Witalise, Spirit. If one increases the tattoo of physical characteristics, it will lower one of the two other physical characteristics. Same thing with the mental characteristics.
Still, you should know that different patterns suitable for some jobs, but not suitable for others. The higher the rank of the profession, the higher the level of a symbol that can be applied.
