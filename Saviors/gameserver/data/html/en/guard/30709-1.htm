Dragon Valley is south of the village. The entrance is located to the west, for Hardin's Academy.
Dragon Valley was so named because it served as the last refuge for the dying dragon. However, one dragon still lives in the Valley. This is the one whose name is called fear - the ancient Earth Dragon Antharas, who chose their relatives graveyard slot.
Many warriors and adventurers tried to defy Antharas, but so far nobody has been able to beat him...
