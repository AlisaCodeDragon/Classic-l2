Managing The Events: *First, You have to collect pieces of hearts.* Part of the hearts you can get from monsters around the village. You will see that the parts are numbered from 1 to 9. Remove each of the numbered parts, and you will be able to participate in the game, *You can play Rock-Scissors-Paper*! This is a simple game, when you win that prizes get yours! After each round you can decide to continue to play or collect prizes and end the game. When you lose, the game ends and you lose your prizes. It is always better to leave after winning.
