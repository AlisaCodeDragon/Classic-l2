Tetrarch Kaitar:
Gran Kain -- The sovereign of destruction, the father of darkness! If the light of the Sun hurts our eyes was made by Einhasad, then the darkness that swallows the light was made by the Dark Lord, the Great Kain. Other races call 'Devil' to the Great Kain, fear him, and exclude their followers.
However, in the afternoon shadows slowly take over the Earth, at that time, the power of the Great Kain surpasses all other Gods -- and when the light is defeated, the flames extinguished, and the winds die, the ground will open and all the rivers and oceans dried up. Then, only darkness will remain...
