A Silver Ranger has similar style as a Human Hawkeye. But their evasion and hit rates are better than Men. He is one of the most dreaded characters during Siege Battle. Even a Paladin would not outlast an attack by a group of them.
They have basic skills such as strengthening their bows and various other skills such as shooting several arrows simultaneously.
To be a Silver Ranger, your level must be over 40 and you need to collect the Mark of the Seeker, the Mark of Life, and the Mark of the Sagittarius.
