Warehouse Chief Reed:
The fundamental characteristics of Scavangers and Artisans are that the Scavanger collects rare materials and the Artisan makes something valuable from those materials. Therefore, a Scavanger must have above average physical strength and combat abilities. He is a great hunter who will brave any danger to collect valuable materials.
The most talented Scavangers are selected to be Master Scavangers. Master Scavangers go to the extreme to find arcane items like dragon scales.
