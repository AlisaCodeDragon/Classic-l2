A Destroyer is a courageous Orc Fighter who carries huge swords and axes at the front line, they are an advanced model of an Orc Raider . They normally carry huge weapons and slay through great numbers of enemies. They usually use polearms during fights with numerous opponents, but they do not succumb during 1 on 1 duels either.
Their basic skills support them to use polearms, swords and clubs. They also have a skill which will increase their offense and defense, if they are in an emergency.
To be a Destroyer, your level must be over 40 and you need to collect the Mark of the Challenger, the Mark of Glory, and the Mark of the Champion.
