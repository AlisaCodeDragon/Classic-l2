Tetrarch Thifiell:
Your level must be at least 20 to be able to change to Palus Knight, and you must complete the quest Test of the Palus Knight.
However, you cannot change profession here. This is because only those who have been known to human society and understand the world beyond the forest are qualified to change profession.
For these reasons, you need to leave this forest, and to venture to the human kingdom of Aden. There is a guild built by the Dark Elves in the town of Gludio, which is a land to the south of here. You must visit the Grand Masters, who are the highest authorities of our members, and completing their searches will allow you to change profession.
Also, there are two villas where you can go from the dark forest. One is the village of Gludin. Although it is the same distance, you have more chances of sobrebivir if you take the road that goes to Scholl of Dark Arts , and pass through the Neutral Zone towards the town of Gludio. However, there can be many Elves in the Neutral Zone, can generate conflicts.
